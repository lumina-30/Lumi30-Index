Decision Record Question

A one-question card for AI review meetings

Status and scope

This is not a demand to stop AI deployment. This is not a certification, legal finding, or safety guarantee.

It is one decision-record question to add to an existing AI review, audit, procurement check, governance meeting, or incident review.

LUMINA-30 does not replace existing AI ethics, AI safety, audit, procurement, security, regulatory, or legal review.

Decision-record question

Before this AI deployment, escalation, or dependency becomes irreversible, can accountable humans effectively refuse, stop, verify, or redirect it?

Answer: [ ] YES  [ ] NO  [ ] UNKNOWN  [ ] NOT RECORDED

If NO or UNKNOWN: What must be resolved before proceeding?

Suggested meeting note

This question does not require the organization to stop. It requires the organization to record whether stopping remains possible before the decision becomes irreversible.

Use this card when reviewing
  • AI deployment
  • AI model upgrade
  • autonomous or agentic AI use
  • procurement of AI systems
  • security or risk review
  • incident review
  • policy or stop-authority review
  • dependency on external AI services
Caution

Do not ask an AI system to decide whether this condition is satisfied. The decision must remain with accountable humans.
