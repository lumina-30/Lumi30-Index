# Explain LUMINA-30 with Your AI

This page lets users copy a prepared prompt so their own AI assistant can explain or evaluate LUMINA-30.

**Important.** LUMINA-30 is not an official standard, legal certification, safety guarantee, government-approved framework, or proof that an AI system is safe.

## How it works

1. Choose a button that matches the desired level or purpose.
2. The explanation prompt is copied.
3. Paste it into your own AI assistant and send it.
4. Ask follow-up questions about LUMINA-30 freely.

The explanation prompt is constructed in this order:

1. Level-specific prompt
2. Common cautions
3. AI Knowledge Pack body

The goal is not to outsource authority to an AI. The goal is to let users read LUMINA-30 through their own AI assistant while avoiding unsupported claims of official status, legal certification, safety guarantees, peer review, institutional adoption, or expert endorsement.

## Level buttons

### Beginner

For people who are new to LUMINA-30, AI governance, or AI safety. The AI should explain the idea briefly and in plain language, using everyday analogies when helpful.

### Practical reviewer

For adoption reviewers, auditors, procurement teams, security reviewers, and AI governance staff. The AI should explain how the boundary condition can be added to existing review, audit, procurement, security, or incident-review workflows.

### Deep review

For readers who want a more detailed structural explanation. The AI should explain the relationships among effective human refusal before irreversibility, PCR-C, Boundary Kernel, incident review, One-Question Pilot, and functional absorption into existing frameworks.

### Critical evaluation

For skeptical review. The AI should separate value, limits, missing validation, practical weaknesses, and overclaiming risks, while not dismissing the framework merely because it is non-official and non-binding.

## Management source

The AI Knowledge Pack body is managed in language-separated Markdown files:

- `ai-knowledge-pack/ai-knowledge-pack-en.md`
- `ai-knowledge-pack/ai-knowledge-pack-ja.md`

The user-facing HTML embeds the corresponding body directly in the page source and concatenates it after the selected level-specific prompt.

## If the AI overstates LUMINA-30

If an AI claims that LUMINA-30 is official, certified, legally binding, widely adopted, peer-reviewed, expert-endorsed, or a safety guarantee, ask it to remove unsupported claims and stay within the copied prompt and linked LUMINA-30 materials.
