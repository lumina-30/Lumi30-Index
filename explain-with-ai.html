Explain LUMINA-30 with Your AI

Copy a level-based prompt and paste it into your own AI.

Important. LUMINA-30 is not an official standard, legal certification, safety guarantee, government-approved framework, or proof that an AI system is safe.

Three steps

This page lets you copy prompts for asking your own AI to explain and evaluate LUMINA-30.

  1. Press the button for the level you prefer. The explanatory prompt will be copied.
  2. Paste it into your AI and send it.
    You can also paste the prompt into a text editor first to review its contents before sending.
  3. Ask freely and continue a conversation with your AI about LUMINA-30.

Choose a level and copy

Beginner

Requests a short, plain-language explanation for readers new to AI governance or LUMINA-30.

Practical reviewer

Requests an adoption, audit, procurement, security, and AI governance evaluation.

Detailed review

Requests a deeper explanation of the core concept, structure, and relation to existing frameworks.

Critical evaluation

Requests separate treatment of strengths, weaknesses, unverified points, and overstatement risks.

If the AI overstates LUMINA-30

If your AI claims that LUMINA-30 is official, certified, legally binding, widely adopted, peer-reviewed, or a safety guarantee, ask it to remove unsupported claims.

This page is a navigation aid. It does not create a new LUMINA-30 framework or status claim.
