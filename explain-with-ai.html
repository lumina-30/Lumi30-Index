Explain LUMINA-30 with Your AI

Copy one ready-to-use prompt, paste it into your AI assistant, and ask it to explain LUMINA-30 without overstating its status.

Important. LUMINA-30 is not an official standard, legal certification, safety guarantee, government-approved framework, or proof that an AI system is safe.

Three steps

  1. Choose beginner or standard explanation below.
  2. Copy the ready-to-use prompt.
  3. Paste it into Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, or another AI assistant.

The goal is not to outsource authority to an AI. The goal is to make the public references easier to understand without overstating LUMINA-30's status.
